Click the button that says Enter, and the program will randomize a Pokemon for you.
To actually see what result(s) you got, you have to check the console.

Step 1: Right click your browser.
Step 2: Click "Inspect" on the menu that comes up.
Step 3: There is a bar with options like "Elements", "Console", "Sources", "Network", etc. Click on the one that says "Console".
